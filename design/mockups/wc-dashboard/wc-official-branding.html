Three takes, one spectrum

B1 — WC mark in navbar

Horizontal "26" mark sits alongside sportspredict wordmark. Co-branding: "this is a World Cup game on sportspredict." Tournament dressing, platform identity preserved.

B2 — WC mark AS the logo

Stacked "26 FIFA" mark IS the primary logo. sportspredict becomes a small secondary label. Strongest WC identity — the app feels like it IS the World Cup. Risk: sportspredict brand disappears.

B3 — Dark WC header band

Dark (#0a0f0a) band with inverted "26" + sportspredict. Feels premium and immersive — like a TV broadcast overlay. The dark band clearly separates "tournament chrome" from "app content." Social strip sits on cream below.

Shared across all three

  • Host city color gradient strip (16 real city colors)
  • Participant avatars with host city bg colors
  • Social CTA card with host-city gradient accent
  • "26" watermark on picks page (3% opacity)
  • Invite icon with amber notification dot
  • Active nav tab underline in host city color

vs Option A (previous prototype)

Option A used a generic rainbow gradient. These options use the actual host city palette and official WC26 marks. Stronger tournament identity. Same social CTA concept.
