Rules

Everything you need to know.

A prediction game built as an homage to the World Cup's own format — groups, knockouts, and a final.

How it works
1
Predict every match
Pick the winner — or exact score for bonus points — before each day's matches lock.
2
Survive the group stage
You're placed in a group of 4. Bottom player after 48 group games is eliminated. Reset. Repeat.
3
Make it to the final
Survivor rounds mirror the World Cup's own bracket. Last one standing wins.
Points
2 pts for correct winner. +3 bonus for exact score. +1 for correct knockout advance.

Knockout rounds
In knockout matches you also predict which team advances. This is separate from the match result — a team can win on penalties and still be your "correct" advance pick.
Format — Survivor
Main game
Survivor-style elimination. You're placed in a group. After each stage, bottom players are eliminated. Points reset. Last one standing wins.
Group Stage
All players enter the tournament split into groups of 4. Each group runs for the duration of the World Cup Group Stage (48 matches). Points accumulate from every match you predict.

Other classifications

Your picks
Each day's matches lock 10 minutes before first kickoff. Change your mind freely before the lock.
Outcome-only predictions
You predict the result of the match — winner or draw — and optionally the exact score. You do not predict individual goalscorers, cards, or other in-match events.
Daily lock
All matches on a given calendar day lock together, 10 minutes before the earliest kickoff. If there are 3 matches on a day and the first starts at 3pm, all 3 lock at 2:50pm. You can edit any pick up until then.
Advance picks
You can submit picks days in advance — no need to log in on matchday. Any pick you've saved before the daily lock counts. Unpicked matches score zero.
Knockout advance picks
In knockout rounds, locks are per-match rather than per-day, as matches are spread across multiple days. Each individual match locks 10 minutes before its own kickoff.
