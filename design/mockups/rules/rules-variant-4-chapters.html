Your Picks

Making predictions

You pick the score for every match in the current round. Here's how it works.

  • 🔒
    Locks 10 min before kickoff
    Each match locks individually, 10 minutes before its scheduled start. The lock is based on the earliest fixture in the round.
  • ✏️
    Change your mind freely
    Until the lock, you can update your picks as many times as you like. Only your final saved prediction counts.
  • ⚽
    Scoreline predictions only
    You predict a final scoreline — no half-time bets, no goalscorers. Extra time and penalties are treated as separate outcomes.
  • 🕐
    Advance picks available
    You can submit picks for any future match as soon as the fixture is published — handy for busy matchdays.

Didn't submit? You'll score 0 for that match. There are no default picks or carry-forwards.
