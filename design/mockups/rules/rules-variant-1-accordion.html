How the cut works
Top 2 in each group auto-qualify for the knockout round.
3rd place enters the danger zone — the 12 third-place finishers are ranked across all groups. Best 8 of 12 advance.
4th place is always eliminated, regardless of points.

Points reset at the start of each knockout round. You compete purely on picks within that stage.

Your picks

Picks lock 10 minutes before the first match of each day. Change your mind as many times as you like until the window closes.

More detail

You only predict the outcome — who wins (or draw) — and optionally the exact scoreline for the bonus. You never pick margins or goal scorers.

Group stage matches lock together at 10 min before the earliest kickoff that day. Knockout matches each have their own lock time.

If you miss the lock window your pick is recorded as a blank — no points, no penalty. A blank on outcome means no score bonus either.

Tiebreakers

Same points? We go down the chain:

H2H goal diff → H2H goals → Overall goal diff → Overall goals → Coin flip
