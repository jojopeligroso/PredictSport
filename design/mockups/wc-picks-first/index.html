WC picks-first redesign — C-series

Stripped FixtureCard, port of /wc/results. Four variants. 2×2 matrix: sectioning × palette.

Anchor: live /wc/results FixtureCard, ported verbatim and stripped of metadata (no venue, no in-city/your-time grid). Kept: group · MD badge, kickoff time, date, home / draw / away pick triple, exact-score inputs.

Selection treatment: amber inset outline on selected team / draw button; amber border on filled score input; amber halo around any card with a saved prediction.

Variables: two axes give a 2×2 grid.
  • Sectioning — by calendar date (chronological) vs by group letter (tournament structure).
  • Palette — proposed 12-hue group accent vs existing host-city palette from src/lib/wc/host-cities.ts.

3-day join cutoff banner is shown on every variant — that's the cron task (#6) made visible.
